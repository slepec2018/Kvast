A bit of history

Origin of kvass Source: pt.wikipedia.org

Kvass appeared in Russia in the X-XI century. The very word "kvass" also has an ancient Russian origin. In addition to Russia, kvass is prepared in Belarus, Serbia, Macedonia, Slovakia and many other countries, but it is called everywhere the same - kvass.


Classification of kvass Source: pt.wikipedia.org

According to Russian GOST for industrial production, this is a drink with a volume fraction of ethyl alcohol of not more than 1.2%, made as a result of incomplete alcoholic and lactic acid fermentation of the wort.
